What's Special?

Burritos are healthy

Burritos are generally made of a flour tortilla, a meat, possibly beans and/or rice, cheese and toppings. Choosing the leanest and most nutrient-dense ingredients make burritos a healthy part of any meal plan.

Better than Tacos

The main difference between a burrito and a taco is the shell size. Tacos are generally a lighter snack or meal, while a larger burrito is a hearty, full meal. Burritos beat out tacos because they're way more versatile.
